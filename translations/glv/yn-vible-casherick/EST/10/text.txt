As hug ree Ahasuerus y cheer, as ellanyn ny marrey fo keesh.
As ooilley obbraghyn e phooar, as e niart, as y stayd ooasle, huggey va'n ree er hroggal Mordecai, nagh vel ad scruit ayns lioar recortyssyn reeaghyn Vedia as Phersia?
Son va Mordecai yn Ew yn fer by-niessey da ree Ahasuerus, as ard mastey ny Hewnyn as soit jeh liorish earroo e chaarjyn, imneagh son maynrys e phobble, as cur er y hoshiaght shee ooilley e chynney.
