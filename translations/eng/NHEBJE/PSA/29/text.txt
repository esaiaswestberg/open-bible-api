Ascribe to Jehovah, you sons of the mighty, ascribe to Jehovah glory and strength.
Ascribe to Jehovah the glory due to his name. Worship Jehovah in holy array.
Jehovah's voice is on the waters. The God of glory thunders, even Jehovah on many waters.
Jehovah's voice is powerful. Jehovah's voice is full of majesty.
The voice of Jehovah breaks the cedars. Yes, Jehovah breaks in pieces the cedars of Lebanon.
He makes them also to skip like a calf; Lebanon and Sirion like a young, wild ox.
Jehovah's voice strikes with flashes of lightning.
Jehovah's voice shakes the wilderness. Jehovah shakes the wilderness of Kadesh.
Jehovah's voice makes the deer calve, and strips the forests bare. In his temple everything says, "Glory!"
Jehovah sat enthroned at the Flood. Yes, Jehovah sits as King forever.
Jehovah will give strength to his people. Jehovah will bless his people with peace.
