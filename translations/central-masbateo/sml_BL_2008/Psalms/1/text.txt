Aheya kahāpan ma a'a sasuku mbal ameya' gara' min a'a jahulaka', sasuku isab mbal pasehe' ma a'a pangandusa. Mbal iya palamud ma saga a'a ya angandali'it Tuhan,
saguwā' kahōpan iya ma sara' ya panoho'anna ma manusiya'. Ya he' kinannal e'na llaw-sangom.
A'a inān sali' sapantun kayu tinanom ma bihing sapa', ya amuwan buwa' ma musimna du. Minsan painay mbal pak'llos dahunna. Ai-ai hinangna, ahāp sadja tungbasna.
Malaingkan saga a'a jahulaka' mbal buwattē'. Sapantun sigām hampa' pai ya tabowa paleyang e' baliyu.
Ya he' sababna angkan saga a'a jahulaka' mbal makara'awa bang ta'abut nihukum, mbal isab pinalamud ma sasuku abontol atayna tudju ni Tuhan.
Saga a'a abontol niayura na pa'in e' PANGHŪ'-Yawe, sagō' saga a'a jahulaka' ala'at sadja kat'kkahan sigām.
